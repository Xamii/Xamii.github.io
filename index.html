What We Do

We’ve got everything you need to handle that case today.

Commercial & Cooporate

We provide adequate cooporate advisory services to various businesses using our vast experience to provide pragmatic and innovative business solutions that aids our clients implementation of effective business practices.

Finance

Our Finance Practice we provide a vast range of legal services to financial institutions, advisers and service regulators, as well as individual and corporate clients across various sectors and industries.

Real Estate

We offer advantages arising from our proven capability in dealing with challenges that arise in large scale corporate finance transaction in the real estate sector. We assist them with Major acquisition, administering & selling down of holding structures. Including construction and environmental matters Letting, sale and leaseback arrangements.

Tax

We provide provide complex transaction structuring tax advice as well as implementation support to clients. Well renowned for our excellent work in representing leading businesses in tax related domestic and international arbitrations, litigation and negotiations against various levels of government in Nigeria.

Energy(Oil & Gas) & Natural Resources

Our expertise spans the entire value chain from upstream exploration and production to oil and gas processing, transportation, commercialization and utilization. We also advise on environmental and regulatory requirements, and assist in formulating and evaluating various methods of Project Financing.
